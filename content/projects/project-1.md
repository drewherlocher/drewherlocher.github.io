## Chyma and the Toll of Time

**Lead Programmer**  
*Unity | Isometric Puzzle-Platformer | Windows | Steam Deck*

A meteor is heading for the planet. Chyma - a small, determined bell — is the only one brave enough to enter the ancient temple that might hold the answer. Using the power of the Rungback, create a Reverb - an echo of yourself that replays your exact movements in real time - and use it to solve puzzles, outsmart enemies, and uncover the story of his people.

As **Lead Programmer**, I was responsible for the core technical systems that bring the game to life, from the enemies that chase you to the lighting that sets the mood.

### My Contributions

- Built AI systems for the game's three enemy types
- Designed and implemented dynamic lighting across all five temple areas
- Wrote the custom shader used for cutout effect as well as general art direction

### Gameplay

- Solve 30+ puzzles across five unique areas
- Create a copy of yourself to be in two places at once
- Outsmart guards, move balls, avoid lasers
- Uncover hidden artifacts that reveal the temple's history
- Dynamic music that reacts to the environment as you play

**Made with:** Unity  
**Genre:** Puzzle-Platformer, Isometric, Free to Play  
**Team:** Sand Storm Studio at Champlain College
